# Infantry — Caravaners (Convoy Riders)

## Subject
A group of nomadic convoy riders in a post-apocalyptic trade caravan setting. Flowing scarves and bandanas wrapped around faces and necks. Light scrap armor — welded metal plates over leather jackets. Bandoliers of ammunition and trade goods slung across chests. Lean, road-worn fighters built for speed and endurance.

## Visual Markers
- Scarves and face wraps (turquoise and copper fabric)
- Light armor — thin welded scrap plates, not bulky
- Bandoliers across the chest
- Slimmer, leaner profile than Engineers
- Goggles (riding/dust protection) hanging around neck or on forehead

## Silhouette
Horizontal emphasis. The scarves billow sideways, bandoliers stretch across the body width. At small sizes, reads as a wider, lower shape — spread out rather than stacked up. Distinctly different from the vertical Engineer silhouette.

## Color Palette
- Primary: copper (#D4845A) — armor plates, leather, bandolier pouches
- Secondary: turquoise (#4ECDC4) — scarves, fabric accents, painted markings
- Accent: sand (#E8D5B7) — base clothing, dust-covered surfaces, light areas

## Art Style
Low-poly 3D model, approximately 300-800 triangles. Vertex colored (colors baked into mesh vertices, no texture maps). Flat shaded, no smoothing groups. Clean geometric shapes — each body part and gear piece is a distinct polygon cluster. Inspired by Kenney City Builder asset style but grittier, weathered, and rougher.

## World Context
Ash & Ember setting — a post-collapse world where a hyper-expansion construction boom stopped overnight. Caravaners use the unfinished infrastructure (half-built highways, abandoned rail lines) as mobile trade routes. Their gear is improvised from scavenged materials — truck parts, road signs, highway reflectors repurposed as armor.

## Camera & Output
- Camera angle: isometric (30 degrees from horizontal)
- Pre-rendered to 2D sprite
- Output: transparent PNG, 128x128 pixels
- Single isometric view, centered in frame
- Lighting: top-left directional, subtle ambient fill
- No ground plane, no shadow blob — transparent background only
