# Infantry — Engineers (Combat Engineers)

## Subject
A squad of combat engineers in a post-apocalyptic industrial setting. Hard hats, tool-laden backpacks, welding goggles pushed up on foreheads. Bulky gear packs and utility belts with wrenches, cutting tools, and measuring instruments. Heavy-duty work boots, reinforced knee pads. One figure carries a pneumatic nail gun repurposed as a weapon.

## Visual Markers
- Hard hats (yellow with hazard stripe band)
- Tool backpacks — oversized, boxy, with protruding implements
- Welding goggles
- Hazard stripe details on shoulders and pack straps
- Bulky, top-heavy silhouette — gear packs dominate the profile

## Silhouette
Vertical emphasis. The gear packs and hard hats make the unit taller and blockier than wide. At small sizes, reads as a chunky upright rectangle with protrusions on top.

## Color Palette
- Primary: yellow (#F2C94C) — hard hats, hazard stripes, pack highlights
- Secondary: steel grey (#828282) — metal tools, reinforced panels, backpack frame
- Accent: black (#1A1A1A) — boots, straps, goggles, deep shadows

## Art Style
Low-poly 3D model, approximately 300-800 triangles. Vertex colored (colors baked into mesh vertices, no texture maps). Flat shaded, no smoothing groups. Clean geometric shapes — each body part and gear piece is a distinct polygon cluster. Inspired by Kenney City Builder asset style but grittier, weathered, and rougher.

## World Context
Ash & Ember setting — a post-collapse world where a hyper-expansion construction boom stopped overnight. Dust covers everything. These engineers are trying to restart civilization's building projects. Their gear is functional but worn, scratched, dented.

## Camera & Output
- Camera angle: isometric (30 degrees from horizontal)
- Pre-rendered to 2D sprite
- Output: transparent PNG, 128x128 pixels
- Single isometric view, centered in frame
- Lighting: top-left directional, subtle ambient fill
- No ground plane, no shadow blob — transparent background only
