# Scout — Engineers (Survey Drone)

## Subject
A small quad-rotor survey drone hovering at low altitude. Four rotor arms extending from a central body in a cross shape. An antenna array mounted on top — multiple thin rods and a small dish. A sensor pod hangs beneath the central body (camera/lidar). The body is a compact box with exposed circuit boards and blinking indicator lights. Small, nimble, utilitarian.

## Visual Markers
- Quad-rotor layout — four arms with rotor discs at each tip
- Antenna array — thin rods and a small dish on top of the body
- Sensor pod — small cylinder or sphere hanging below
- Cross-shaped silhouette from above
- Compact central body — boxy, industrial, no curves

## Silhouette
Vertical emphasis (even though small). The antenna array extends upward from the body, making the vertical dimension slightly dominant. At small sizes, reads as a small cross shape with a dot on top — distinctly different from the Caravaner Motorbike's horizontal line.

## Color Palette
- Primary: yellow (#F2C94C) — rotor arms, body panels, hazard markings
- Secondary: steel grey (#828282) — antenna array, sensor pod, structural frame
- Accent: black (#1A1A1A) — rotors, circuit board, deep shadows

## Art Style
Low-poly 3D model, approximately 300-800 triangles. Vertex colored (colors baked into mesh vertices, no texture maps). Flat shaded, no smoothing groups. Clean geometric shapes — rotor arms as flat rectangles, body as a box, antenna as thin cylinders. Inspired by Kenney City Builder asset style but grittier and more utilitarian.

## World Context
Ash & Ember setting. Originally a construction site survey drone used to map terrain and inspect unfinished structures. Now repurposed by Engineers for battlefield reconnaissance. Scratched plastic casing, dust on the lens, one rotor arm slightly bent but functional.

## Camera & Output
- Camera angle: isometric (30 degrees from horizontal)
- Pre-rendered to 2D sprite
- Output: transparent PNG, 128x128 pixels
- Single isometric view, centered in frame
- Lighting: top-left directional, subtle ambient fill
- No ground plane, no shadow blob — transparent background only
