# Artillery — Caravaners (Rocket Truck)

## Subject
A medium truck with a rocket launcher array mounted on its flatbed. The cab is armored with welded scrap plates. The truck bed carries a tilted rack of rocket tubes — multiple cylindrical barrels arranged in a grid, angled upward at approximately 45 degrees. The truck sits on heavy all-terrain wheels. Cloth tarps partially cover the rocket rack when not firing. Exhaust pipe on the side of the cab.

## Visual Markers
- Truck cab — armored with welded scrap, rounded but angular low-poly shape
- Rocket rack — grid of cylindrical tubes angled upward from the truck bed
- Angled launcher array — the dominant visual feature, tilted at 45 degrees
- Heavy wheels — oversized all-terrain tires
- Cloth tarp — partially draped over the rocket rack (turquoise fabric)

## Silhouette
Horizontal emphasis. The truck body stretches wide, and the angled rocket rack extends the horizontal profile further. At small sizes, reads as a wide shape with an angled protrusion (the launcher). Lower and wider than the Engineer Crane Cannon's tall vertical frame.

## Color Palette
- Primary: copper (#D4845A) — truck cab, welded armor plates, rocket tube housings
- Secondary: turquoise (#4ECDC4) — tarp over rocket rack, painted convoy markings, accent stripes
- Accent: sand (#E8D5B7) — truck bed floor, tire sidewalls, dust-covered surfaces

## Art Style
Low-poly 3D model, approximately 300-800 triangles. Vertex colored (colors baked into mesh vertices, no texture maps). Flat shaded, no smoothing groups. Distinct geometric parts: cab, flatbed, rocket tube array, wheels, tarp. Inspired by Kenney City Builder asset style but grittier, rougher, and improvised-looking.

## World Context
Ash & Ember setting. Caravaners mounted salvaged rocket tubes onto a cargo truck. The rockets were originally construction demolition charges — meant to clear rock for tunnel boring. Now they clear enemy positions from a distance. The truck doubles as a supply hauler when the rockets aren't loaded. Road grime, dented fenders, mismatched wheel sizes.

## Camera & Output
- Camera angle: isometric (30 degrees from horizontal)
- Pre-rendered to 2D sprite
- Output: transparent PNG, 128x128 pixels
- Single isometric view, centered in frame
- Lighting: top-left directional, subtle ambient fill
- No ground plane, no shadow blob — transparent background only
