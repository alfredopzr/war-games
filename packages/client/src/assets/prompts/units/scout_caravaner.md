# Scout — Caravaners (Motorbike)

## Subject
A dirt bike / scrambler motorcycle ridden by a goggled scout. The bike is stripped-down and functional — exposed engine, knobby off-road tires, welded scrap fender. The rider leans forward over the handlebars, wearing goggles and a scarf trailing behind. Saddlebags or a small cargo rack on the rear. Fast, light, built for rough terrain scouting.

## Visual Markers
- Dirt bike frame — exposed engine block, high exhaust pipe, knobby tires
- Rider with goggles and trailing scarf
- Thin, elongated profile
- Small cargo rack or saddlebags on the rear
- No heavy armor — speed over protection

## Silhouette
Horizontal emphasis. The bike and rider create a long, low, narrow shape stretched across the frame. At small sizes, reads as a thin horizontal line with a small bump (rider). Distinctly different from the Engineer Drone's cross shape.

## Color Palette
- Primary: copper (#D4845A) — bike frame, engine block, rider's jacket
- Secondary: turquoise (#4ECDC4) — scarf, painted stripes on the tank, goggle lenses
- Accent: sand (#E8D5B7) — tires (dust-covered), saddlebags, rider's pants

## Art Style
Low-poly 3D model, approximately 300-800 triangles. Vertex colored (colors baked into mesh vertices, no texture maps). Flat shaded, no smoothing groups. Distinct geometric parts: wheels, frame, engine, rider body, handlebars. Inspired by Kenney City Builder asset style but grittier, weathered, and road-worn.

## World Context
Ash & Ember setting. Caravaners use motorbikes as fast scouts, racing ahead of the convoy on abandoned highways to check for hazards, find routes through collapsed overpasses, and spot rival factions. The bike is cobbled together from scavenged parts — mismatched but reliable.

## Camera & Output
- Camera angle: isometric (30 degrees from horizontal)
- Pre-rendered to 2D sprite
- Output: transparent PNG, 128x128 pixels
- Single isometric view, centered in frame
- Lighting: top-left directional, subtle ambient fill
- No ground plane, no shadow blob — transparent background only
