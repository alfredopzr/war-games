# Tank — Caravaners (War Rig Truck)

## Subject
An armored semi-truck converted into a mobile war platform — a war rig. Long horizontal body with a reinforced cab at the front and a weaponized flatbed at the rear. Dual exhaust stacks rising from behind the cab belching dark smoke. Welded scrap armor plates covering the entire body — mismatched metals, bolted and riveted together. Heavy wheels (not tracks), oversized tires for rough terrain.

## Visual Markers
- Armored semi-truck silhouette — long, horizontal, road-dominant
- Exhaust stacks — twin vertical pipes behind the cab
- Welded plates — patchwork scrap armor, different metal colors, visible welds
- Oversized wheels — rugged all-terrain tires
- Cloth banners or flags tied to the antenna or exhaust stacks (turquoise fabric)

## Silhouette
Horizontal emphasis. The long truck body stretches wide across the frame. At small sizes, reads as a long, low rectangle — distinctly wider than tall. The exhaust stacks provide minor vertical accent but the dominant read is horizontal length. Clearly different from the vertical Engineer Siege Vehicle.

## Color Palette
- Primary: copper (#D4845A) — welded armor plates, cab body, rust tones
- Secondary: turquoise (#4ECDC4) — painted markings, banners, accent stripes
- Accent: sand (#E8D5B7) — dust-covered surfaces, lighter armor patches, tire sidewalls

## Art Style
Low-poly 3D model, approximately 300-800 triangles. Vertex colored (colors baked into mesh vertices, no texture maps). Flat shaded, no smoothing groups. Distinct geometric parts: cab, flatbed, wheels, exhaust stacks, armor plates. Inspired by Kenney City Builder asset style but grittier, weathered, and road-worn.

## World Context
Ash & Ember setting. This was a commercial hauling truck before the collapse. The Caravaners welded scrap armor onto it, mounted weapons on the flatbed, and turned it into a mobile fortress. It travels the half-finished highways and abandoned rail corridors as part of trade convoys. Road dust, oil stains, dented panels.

## Camera & Output
- Camera angle: isometric (30 degrees from horizontal)
- Pre-rendered to 2D sprite
- Output: transparent PNG, 128x128 pixels
- Single isometric view, centered in frame
- Lighting: top-left directional, subtle ambient fill
- No ground plane, no shadow blob — transparent background only
